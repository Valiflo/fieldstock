How it works

One system. Three steps.

Every transaction in FieldStock follows the same simple chain — from the moment stock arrives to the moment it's used on a job.

Step 01
Warehouse
Stock is received, cataloged, and managed by your warehouse manager. Every item gets a barcode.
Step 02
Truck
Parts are transferred to a truck. A tech checks out the truck and takes ownership of what's on it.
Step 03
Job
The tech scans or enters what was used on the job, tied to a work order number. Stock updates instantly.
Features

Everything the team needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

FieldStock is purpose-built for field service inventory — not adapted from a generic warehouse tool.

📦
Item Catalog
Manage all your parts with internal IDs, descriptions, vendor part numbers, costs, and supplier info. Designed to scale from 100 to 10,000+ SKUs.
🔖
Barcode Generation & Printing
Automatically generate barcodes for items that don't have them. Print label sheets directly from the system — no separate software needed.
📥
Receiving
Log incoming shipments item by item. Scan barcodes, confirm quantities, and warehouse stock updates automatically. Attach a PO reference number for your records.
🚚
Truck Check-In / Check-Out
Techs check out a truck at the start of the day and check it in when done. While a truck is checked out, that tech is accountable for everything on it.
🔄
Warehouse-to-Truck Transfers
Stock a truck from the warehouse with a simple scan-and-confirm workflow. Inventory deducts from the warehouse and adds to the truck in real time.
↔️
Truck-to-Truck Transfers
When a tech needs to borrow from another truck, it's logged — not lost. Every transfer has a record with who, what, when, and how many.
📱
Mobile Job Consumption
Techs open the site on their phone, enter a work order number, and scan or select what they used. Takes about 60 seconds at the end of a job.
📸
Camera Barcode Scanning
No scanner hardware needed. Techs use the phone camera to scan any item barcode in the field — during a job or at the end of the day.
📋
Par List Management
Set standard stocking levels per item per truck. The system uses these to flag when a truck is running low and guide restocking decisions.
🔁
Returns
Unused parts can be returned from a truck back to the warehouse — tracked, timestamped, and credited back to warehouse stock.
✏️
Manual Adjustments
Warehouse managers can correct quantities at any location with a required reason note. Every adjustment is logged — nothing disappears silently.
🔍
Cycle Counts
Run a physical count of the warehouse or any truck. The system shows expected quantities, you enter actuals, and it highlights any discrepancies before you post.
User Roles

The right tools for
the right people.

Each role sees only what they need — no clutter, no confusion.

Warehouse Manager
  • Receive and catalog new stock
  • Transfer stock to trucks
  • View all truck stock levels
  • Run low-stock reports
  • Conduct cycle counts
  • Print barcode labels
  • Process returns & adjustments
Field Technician
  • Check out and check in a truck
  • View current stock on their truck
  • Scan items to log job consumption
  • Enter work order number on each job
  • Initiate truck-to-truck transfers
Admin / Owner
  • Full access to all features
  • Manage users and roles
  • Access all reports and cost data
  • Override or force-close assignments
  • Manage par lists and catalog
Reporting

Know where everything is.
Know what it cost.

Every transaction is recorded. Every report is exportable to CSV or PDF.

Current Stock by Location
Real-time snapshot of every item at the warehouse and each truck, with quantities and par levels.
Low Stock / Reorder Report
Items at or below reorder threshold. The manager's input to building a purchase order — ready to export.
Consumption by Work Order
Every item used on a specific job — quantities, unit costs, extended costs. Answers: what did this job actually cost?
Consumption by Technician
Material usage summary per tech over any date range. Supports accountability and productivity visibility.
Usage Trends by Item
How fast is each SKU moving? Surfaces high-velocity items that may need higher par levels.
Full Transaction History
Complete audit log — filterable by type, location, item, user, or date range. Nothing is ever deleted.
Truck Stock Summary
At-a-glance view of all trucks: who has it checked out, total items stocked, how many are below par.
Variance / Shrinkage Report
Expected vs. actual counts from audit sessions. Identifies loss patterns over time.
